Our Mission

At On Point Remediation, our mission is to provide safe, effective, and thorough mold remediation services to homeowners and businesses in the KC Metro Area. What started as 2 men determined to make their mark in the remediation industry has grown into a trusted name in the industry, serving in the KC Metro Area for over a year now we have grown exponentially and plan on continuing to make a difference for our clients in a meaningful way. So while we've indeed expanded our services and team, we've never lost sight of our core values: quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
