Our Services

Comprehensive mold remediation and related services for your home or business.

Mold Remediation

Our comprehensive mold remediation service addresses the root cause of mold growth and safely removes all traces of mold from your property. We follow industry-standard protocols to ensure thorough remediation.

Our certified technicians use advanced equipment and environmentally friendly products to eliminate mold while minimizing disruption to your home or business.

Our Process Includes:

  • Thorough inspection and assessment
  • Containment to prevent cross-contamination
  • HEPA air filtration
  • Safe removal of mold-infested materials
  • Cleaning and sanitizing affected areas
  • Post-remediation verification

Mold Inspection & Testing

Our professional mold inspection services help identify hidden mold problems before they become serious health hazards or cause structural damage to your property.

We use advanced detection methods and laboratory testing to accurately identify mold species and concentration levels, allowing us to develop an effective remediation plan.

Our Inspection Services Include:

  • Visual inspection of all areas
  • Moisture mapping and detection
  • Air quality sampling
  • Surface sampling
  • Thermal imaging to detect hidden moisture
  • Detailed report with findings and recommendations

Water Damage Restoration

Water damage is often the primary cause of mold growth. Our water damage restoration services address both the immediate damage and help prevent future mold problems

We respond quickly to water emergencies to minimize damage and begin the drying process as soon as possible, using industrial-grade equipment and proven techniques.

Our Water Damage Services Include:

  • Emergency water extraction
  • Structural drying
  • Dehumidification
  • Sanitizing and deodorizing
  • Structural repairs
  • Preventative treatments to inhibit mold growth

Indoor Air Quality Improvement

Poor indoor air quality can lead to health issues and discomfort. Our air quality improvement services help create a healthier indoor environment by removing contaminants and improving ventilation.

We offer comprehensive solutions to address various air quality concerns, from mold spores and allergens to VOCs and other pollutants.

Our Air Quality Services Include:

  • Air quality testing and assessment
  • HVAC system cleaning and sanitizing
  • Air duct cleaning
  • Air purification systems installation
  • Ventilation improvment
  • Odor removal and deodorization

Preventative Maintenance

The best way to deal with mold is to prevent it from growing in the first place. Our preventative maintenance programs help protect your property from mold and moisture problems.

We offer regular inspections, moisture control solutions, and preventative treatments to keep your home or business mold-free.

Our Preventative Services Include:

  • Regular moisture inspections
  • Humidity control solutions
  • Waterproofing services
  • Crawl space encapsulation
  • Antimicrobial treatments
  • Customized maintenance plans

Wood Repair

Wood damage from moisture or age can compromise the integrity and aesthetics of your home. Our wood repair services restore damaged structures while preserving their original look and strength.

We work with a variety of wood types and finishes to deliver seamless repairs that blend beautifully with your space.

Our Wood Repair Services Include:

  • Dry rot repair and treatment
  • Exterior siding and trim restoration
  • Deck and porch structural repairs
  • Interior framing and support fixes
  • Custom carpentry for restoration
  • Protective sealing and finishing

Home Remodeling (Interior & Exterior)

From refreshing interiors to upgrading your home’s exterior, our remodeling services are tailored to bring your vision to life. We combine skilled craftsmanship with functional design to enhance comfort, style, and property value.

Whether you’re dreaming of a new kitchen, a bathroom overhaul, or exterior curb appeal, we’re here to help.

Our Remodeling Services Include:

  • Kitchen and bathroom renovations
  • Flooring and drywall installation
  • Painting and lighting upgrades
  • Exterior siding and paint
  • Window and door replacements
